NAV Price Floor

Asset-backed NAV floor. Each token represents a share of the oil reserve fund’s net asset value. Tokens can be burned to redeem NAV in USDT.

GENESIS SUPPLY: 1B TQOR

Deflation

Redeemed tokens are permanently burned, reducing total supply. Trading profits also fund open-market buybacks and burns.
